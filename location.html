Located in the heart of Malacca

Mövenpick Hotel & Apartments Bur Dubai, situated close to key commercial areas, famous shopping malls and key attractions in Oud Metha, a central business district in Bur Dubai. Several well known spots can be reached within 10 minutes, including Dubai Mall and Dubai Fountain, world’s biggest Dubai Frame and tallest Burj Khalifa, Wafi mall, Dubai World Trade Centre and Dubai International Airport. Make yourself at home in our 255 luxury hotel rooms and suites, or choose one of 57 stylish hotel apartments, with a kitchen and up to three bedrooms. We are pleased to offer complimentary Wi-Fi in all rooms, each thoughtfully designed with its own artworks and decor. Executive Lounge access is included for some rooms. In our 5-star Dubai hotel you can enjoy everything from North Indian and Lebanese cuisine, to international flavours and poolside refreshments in our seven restaurants and bars, as well as a nightclub. Unwind by the azure waters of the rooftop PoolHouse or fitness centre, enjoy a treatment at the salon, spa or simply marvel at the art showcased around the hotel.
